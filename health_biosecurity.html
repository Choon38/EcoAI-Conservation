Health & Biosecurity

Implementing protocols to protect plants from pests and diseases is crucial for the health of our ecosystems.

Our Initiatives

  • Developing integrated pest management strategies to minimize damage to plant species.
  • Conducting research on plant diseases to improve early detection and treatment.
  • Establishing guidelines for biosecurity to prevent the introduction of invasive species.

The Importance of Health & Biosecurity

Health and biosecurity are essential components of conservation. Protecting plants from threats like pests and diseases ensures the survival of species and the stability of ecosystems.

Get Involved

You can support health and biosecurity initiatives by advocating for sustainable agricultural practices, participating in local biosecurity efforts, and educating others about the importance of plant health.
